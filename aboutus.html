About Us

Web Wallet is a website that allows customer to purchase items from the wet market, through the use of a digital wallet on the site. Customers can either visit the market physically and place an order for their desired items and making payment through their digital wallet on the website. Additionally, the transactions made through the digital wallet will be written to a blockchain, making it secure and immutable.

This makes us your best and most trusted choice for all your market shopping needs!

How do we work?

To begin, go to the Web Wallet website. If you already have an account, you can log in using your credentials. If you don't have an account, you can sign up for one. Once logged in, you can browse the available items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Payments can be made through your digital wallet, and all transactions are securely recorded on the blockchain.
